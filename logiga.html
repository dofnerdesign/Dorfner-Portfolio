løgigå corporate design

2023

LØGIGÅ, a fictional premium Swedish brand, pioneers sustainable fashion by crafting high-quality, mostly recyclable fabrics. With a mission to transform fashion consumption, the initiative encourages a "slow-fashion lifestyle" through the mantra "change your style, change the world". LØGIGÅ envisions a collective shift towards sustainability, empowering individuals to contribute to a greener future, redefining fashion for a more responsible and eco-conscious tomorrow.
