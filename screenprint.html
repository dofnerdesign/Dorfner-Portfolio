screenprinted poster

2023

This poster was designed and crafted in another screenprinting class in my 2nd semester. Since New York City has proven to own part of my heart more than once, I dedicated this poster to my adventures and wonderful memories in the city that never sleeps. Shown is the origin of every traveller’s memories, Grand Central Station, marking the heart of Manhattan’s city map.
