experimental typography

2023

While designing folders and choosing from different layouts is one thing, experimenting with text and letter forms in detail is a whole other story. This folder was created around the topic “tongue twisters” and helped with developing an understanding of letter spacing and sizing, and how the placement of text affects its readability. Paired with some of Klabund’s poems, it ended up making a fun and extraordinarily shaped folder.
