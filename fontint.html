font interpretation

2023

This graphic design project was aiming to present and highlight the intricacies of the typeface 'Tomato Grotesk' by the Italian type designer Andrea Biggio. The goal was to create both a poster and a folder that effectively showcase the versatility of this font. The project involved custom illustrations, a detailed analysis of the assigned type designer's work, and a thoughtful exploration of the unique characteristics of the 'Tomato Grotesk' typeface.
