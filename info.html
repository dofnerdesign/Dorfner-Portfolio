information design

2023

In this information design project, I thoroughly investigated 26 toothpaste products within the same category, exploring their flavors, ingredients, and prices. Through systematic data collection, I then employed three distinct visual styles to represent this information, creating informative and visually striking posters. Focusing on the nuances of toothpaste, the project aimed to convey a comprehensive understanding of flavors, ingredients, and pricing within the chosen category.
