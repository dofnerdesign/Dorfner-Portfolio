identity crisis

2023

This project involved coding a generative fingerprint and designing an interactive poster. The dynamic fingerprint, inspired by the uniqueness of real fingerprints, changes shape with user interaction. The core message encourages people to celebrate their individuality rather than conforming to societal expectations. The interactive poster serves as a metaphor, urging viewers to take pride in their distinctive qualities.
