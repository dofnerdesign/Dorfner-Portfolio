windows to the soul

2023

For this project, I focused on capturing various emotions solely through the detailed portrayal of the eyes. Using pencil as my medium, I paid special attention to accurately rendering the pupil and its reflections. Each drawing aims to convey a range of emotions, showcasing the depth and complexity of human feelings with precision and authenticity.
