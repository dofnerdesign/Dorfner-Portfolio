song poster design

2022

In my poster design project centered around Miley Cyrus's "The Climb," I aimed to visually represent life's journey of ups and downs. The first poster embodies the daily "mountains" we navigate, featuring the song's lyrics. The second poster, a dreamy sunset scene, signifies the triumph after overcoming challenges. Together, these posters encapsulate the resilience and determination encouraged by the song, inspiring viewers to persist and rise stronger after every fall.
