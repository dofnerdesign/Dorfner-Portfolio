generative business cards

2023

During my second semester at New Design University we started getting into coding. Our first project was to design & code a generative business card for our own, potential, design agency. By using a randomized grid, I created a pattern consisting of corner arcs and circles, varying in colors from 8 different color palettes to create a different, one of a kind pattern for every single card.
